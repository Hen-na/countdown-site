MINECRAFT LIVE?

Tune in to our epic livestream for the newest news, deep dives, and the reveal of the mob vote winner! This year, you can vote for a new mob on Minecraft.net, in the launcher, or by playing our special carnival map for Bedrock Edition. Vote for the glare!
  • October 16 at noon EDT!
  • The glare warns you when you’re in an area that is dark enough for monsters to spawn! How does it let you know? By getting super grumpy, of course! Vote for the glare during Minecraft Live

Vote for the copper golem!
  • October 16 at noon EDT!
